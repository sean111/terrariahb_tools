The Clothier is an NPC vendor. The "Clothier" is actually the Old Man. After you defeat Skeletron, you will have freed the Old Man from his curse. The Old Man will be gone and will arrive shortly as the Clothier if all requirements are met. His clothes are no longer tattered and he is wearing a red hat.
