Forests are the main part of the surface world. The landscape of a forest includes lots of grass, plenty of trees, several lakes ranging from small to large and, sometimes, Sunflowers. There is a small chance for Copper Ore and Iron Ore to spawn on the surface in a forest. The initial spawn point is always on a forest.

Monsters in Forests

  • During the day:
    • Slimes
  • During the night:
    • [[Zombie]]
    • [[Demon Eye]]

Spawn Rates

  • During the day:
    • 100% speed
    • ~4 max
  • During the night:
    • 166% speed
    • ~5 max
  • During a Blood Moon:
    • 333% speed
    • ~7 max
  • NPC towns can lower these rates
