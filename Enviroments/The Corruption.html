The Corruption is a dark area where corruption has spread over dirt blocks and trees. This area will have thorns which will hurt the player as well and dangerous chasms that can kill you if you blindly enter them. The chasms tend to contain Shadow Orbs and are made of Ebonstone Blocks. These blocks can only be destroyed by the Nightmare Pickaxe, Molten Pickaxe or Dynamite.
